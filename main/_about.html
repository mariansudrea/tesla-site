Tesla Solutions is a thriving electrical contracting company based in the San Francisco Bay Area, committed to delivering green and value-driven solutions. By providing the best in energy-efficient equipment, our goal is to do good for our clients, the planet and its people, while building a business that creates jobs in our community.
About Tesla Solutions
Tesla Solutions was established in 2004 by a group of seasoned Bay Area electrical professionals. Our core values combine environmental friendliness, a movement towards clean and alternative energy sources, a dedication to customer satisfaction, and a focus on long-term value for our clients.

We serve the San Francisco Bay Area, as well as communities within a 30-mile radius. We are licensed, bonded and insured California contractors, providing complete electrical solutions, including LED lighting installation, home automation sytems, electrical retrofitting and many more.

In collaboration with PG&E and local energy companies, we deliver a number of incentive and rebate programs. These programs are available to commercial, industrial and agricultural businesses who wish to install eligible products. We believe that value and ecological sustainability go hand-in-hand, and we take pride in providing solutions that yield lasting benefits for both the environment and our clients.
Our values
Environmentally friendly
We specialize in the design and installation of clean and energy-efficient systems. By bringing this technology to our clients, we hope to make environmentally friendly contracting the norm and create a more sustainable community one building at a time.

Traditional systems often have a high environmental impact. Older or inefficient electrical and HVAC systems are a significant drain on our nation's energy resources, and standard commercial refrigeration systems generate large quantities of greenhouse gases.

The components we install are engineered and proven to be better for the environment. Our team of knowledgeable professionals is committed to designing systems that will address the whole building and minimize the client's carbon footprint.

Value-Driven, Complete Electrical Solutions
Energy efficiency means more than just installing new appliances. Upgrading a building's electrical system can also decrease energy costs substantially.

As certified electrical contractors, we work with our clients to design electrical systems to meet their needs, and provide lighting and wiring upgrades, as well as low-voltage fixtures and automation systems. Currently, benefit and incentive programs are available to many businesses, making this the ideal time to upgrade an electrical system.
Name-Brand Components
In order to deliver true value to our clients, we build systems that last. The components we use are durable and high-quality products from trusted manufacturers. This ensures that the solutions we install perform as intended, and minimizes opportunities for energy inefficiency.
Manufacturer's Warranty
Customer satisfaction is our primary objective, and we know that a client who chooses us is making an investment in energy efficiency. We protect our clients' investments by offering items with a manufacturer's warranty. Manufacturers include Sylvania, ElectraLED and others.
